### Read the score — "why is this not 0.0?"

A deficit score is never a black box. Every file attributes its score back to the
four measurement dimensions, so you can see *which* axis is dragging it down.

| Band | Score | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|
| ✅ CLEAN | < 30 | Healthy structure |
| ⚠️ SUSPICIOUS | 30–50 | Worth a look |
| 🔶 INFLATED | 50–70 | Action before merge |
| 🚨 CRITICAL | ≥ 70 | Immediate deep-dive |

Penalty attribution (higher = worse, sums to the total) tells you the top driver
at a glance: inflation, low logic density, unused imports, or critical patterns.
